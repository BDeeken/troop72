About Us

We are Scouts BSA Troop 72 out of Park City, Utah. We range in age from 11-17. Our adult leadership is Scoutmaster David Wickersham, and scout leadership roles are shown further down the page. Every meeting is fun and the Scouts are very nice. We meet every Tuesday from 6 to 7 PM and we have many campouts throughout the year. If you are interested in joining our Troop, please call David Wickersham (571)228-9153.
